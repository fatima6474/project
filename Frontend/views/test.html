FAQ

Explore frequently asked questions and find answers to common queries in this section.

How can I hire a local talent on Skill Work Community?

Answer

To hire a local talent, visit the "Find Talent" page, choose the category that suits your needs, and browse through available profiles. Contact the talent directly through their profile to discuss further details.

What information should I include in my Skill Work Community profile?

Answer

Include details about your skills, experience, and any relevant certifications. Make sure to upload a professional profile picture and provide contact information so that clients can easily reach out to you.

Is my personal information secure on Skill Work Community?

Answer

Yes, we take the security of your information seriously. Our platform uses encryption and follows best practices to protect your data.

Can I connect with multiple talents for different services?

Answer

Absolutely! You can connect with as many talents as you need for various services. Each connection is independent of others.

How do I post a job as a client on Skill Work Community?

Answer

To post a job, log in to your account, go to the "Post a Job" section, and fill out the necessary details about the job. Once submitted, talents can apply for your job.

Can talents on Skill Work Community work remotely?

Answer

Yes, talents have the option to specify whether they can work remotely. You can filter talents based on your preference for remote or on-site work when searching for services.

What should I do if I encounter issues with a talent's service?

Answer

If you encounter issues, contact the talent directly through the messaging system on Skill Work Community. If the issue persists, you can reach out to our support team for assistance.

How do I reset my password if I forget it?

Answer

On the login page, click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address to reset your password.

Are there any fees for using Skill Work Community?

Answer

There are no fees for clients. Talents may be subject to service fees based on the terms outlined in their profiles and agreements.

How do I leave a review for a talent?

Answer

After completing a service, you can leave a review on the talent's profile. Click on the talent's profile, scroll down to the review section, and share your feedback.

Is there a mobile app for Skill Work Community?

Answer

Currently, Skill Work Community is accessible through a web browser. We are working on a mobile app for a better user experience on smartphones.

Can I change my username on Skill Work Community?

Answer

Currently, the option to change your username is not available. Choose a username carefully during the registration process.

How can I verify the skills and qualifications of a talent?

Answer

You can verify a talent's skills and qualifications by reviewing their profile, including any certifications and past work experience. Additionally, you can request references or samples of their work.
